Who We Are

About Institute of
Digital Risk

The Institute of Digital Risk (IDR) is an industry-led institute focused on training and deploying professionals in cyber risk, technology risk, and digital governance roles.

IDR was established to meet the growing global demand for skilled digital risk professionals. We work directly with industry leaders to design and deliver programmes that are immediately applicable in the real world — spanning financial services, critical infrastructure, and emerging technology sectors.

Our approach bridges the gap between academic theory and professional practice, ensuring every graduate is equipped to step into high-impact roles from day one.
